Lift Access System

Restrict lift access.
Protect shared facilities.

Integration to existing elevators to prohibit defaulters from using common facilities.

Link lift access directly to each unit's payment status in iRuma. Defaulters are automatically restricted from floor access until arrears are cleared — no manual intervention required.

  • Integrates with existing elevator hardware — no full replacement needed
  • Auto-restricts defaulter units based on outstanding charges in iRuma
  • Card, fob, or QR access modes — choose what suits your residents
  • Auto-reinstates access immediately upon payment — no manual override needed
  • Full lift usage logs per unit for JMB audit and reporting

Barrier Gate & Long Range RFID

Faster entry.
Zero unauthorised vehicles.

Integration with LPR / ANPR to prohibit defaulters and enable faster visitor vehicle recognition.

iRuma's barrier gate integration combines long-range RFID with licence plate recognition so resident vehicles pass through without stopping — while defaulter vehicles are automatically denied.

  • LPR / ANPR — auto-reads plates for contactless resident entry
  • Long-range RFID — detects sticker-tagged vehicles from 3–10 metres away
  • Automatically blocks defaulter vehicles based on iRuma arrears status
  • Visitor pre-registration via resident app generates a one-time vehicle pass
  • Full vehicle entry/exit log with timestamps and captured plate images
EntryPass Microengine ZKTeco LPR / ANPR Camera
Face Time Attendance System

Accurate attendance.
Live in iRuma.

Records cleaners, guards and management office staff attendance via facial recognition with real-time reporting in iRuma.

Staff clock in and out using their face — eliminating buddy-punching and manual timesheets. All data syncs instantly to iRuma's Daily Attendance dashboard.

  • Facial recognition clock-in/out — no cards, no PINs, no proxy attendance
  • Real-time Daily Attendance Report visible directly in iRuma system
  • Covers cleaners, security guards, and management office staff in one system
  • Automated lateness and absence alerts sent to property management
  • Exportable attendance summaries for payroll processing and auditing
ZKTeco Microengine Real-time sync to iRuma
M&E Common Facilities IoT

Automate your building.
Cut M&E waste effortlessly.

Common Facilities IoT to automatically switch on & off the lights, aircons or any other M&E equipment.

Connect your common-area lights, air conditioners, and mechanical & electrical equipment to iRuma's IoT gateway. Sensors and schedules handle the switching — so your staff don't have to.

  • Automated on/off scheduling for lights, aircons, and M&E equipment
  • Motion/occupancy sensors — equipment activates only when areas are in use
  • Smart curtain & dimmer integration for lobby and meeting room ambience control
  • IoT Gateway unifies all device signals into a single performance monitoring dashboard
  • Remote on/off override from the iRuma platform — control any device from anywhere
