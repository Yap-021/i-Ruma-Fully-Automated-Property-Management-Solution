Privacy Policy

I-Ruma Sdn Bhd (202101016476) (1416776-K) — compliant with Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act 2010.

🕐 Updated: 14 Apr 2021
I-Ruma is an intelligent property management solution. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and process your Personal Data in compliance with Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA). By using our App or Site, you consent to the practices described herein.

We may collect Personal Data from you through various means, including when you:

  • Register and create an account on our App or Site
  • Submit any form relating to our products and services
  • Download or access the App
  • Agree for the App to access your location
  • Scan QR codes on our platform
  • Participate in promotions or website features
  • Request product or service information
  • Carry out transactions through our App or Site
  • Make enquiries or interact with our staff

We may also automatically collect information via cookies, web beacons, analytical tags, web server logs, geo-location technologies, and QR codes.

You are not obliged to provide Personal Data, but failure to do so may mean we cannot provide certain services to you.

We do not transact through the App or Site directly with anyone we know to be under the age of 18. If you are under 18, you should use the App or Site only with parental or guardian involvement and should not submit any Personal Data to us. By providing any Personal Data, you declare that you are over 18.

To use certain functionalities of the I-Ruma Product, you must create a user account. Registration requires your name, contact number, email address, username, and password. We may also ask for additional details such as shipping address, photo identification, bank account details, date of birth, gender, and interests.

Your username and password provide secure access to your account — keep your password strictly confidential.

When you browse our Site, your computer sends information including your IP address, operating system, browser name/version, referring website, requested page, date/time, and cookies. This information may be associated with your personal account if logged in, and used in anonymous statistics to understand how visitors use our Site.

We collect and process your Personal Data for one or more of the following purposes:

  • Administering and facilitating your use of the App or Site
  • Monitoring and improving your experience on the App or Site
  • Managing QR code-based registration and contact tracing
  • Providing and improving our services; remembering preferences
  • Tailoring your experience based on interests and preferences
  • Assessing and processing subscription or purchase requests
  • Registering you as a user and managing your account
  • Processing transactions and delivering products/services
  • Responding to enquiries and customer service complaints
  • Contacting you via phone, email, text, or postal mail
  • Investigating fraud, unlawful activity, or misconduct
  • Complying with applicable law and regulatory requirements
  • Conducting research, surveys, market analysis, and data analytics
  • Storing, hosting, and backing up your Personal Data
  • Marketing and sending promotional materials (in compliance with PDPA)
  • Maintaining information technology systems
  • Producing internal statistics and statutory reports

In conducting our business, we may disclose your Personal Data to third parties for one or more of the stated Purposes. Such third parties include:

  • Our related corporations and affiliates, locally or overseas
  • Agents, contractors, or third-party service providers (e.g., mailing, telecoms, logistics, IT companies)
  • Business partners
  • Authorities where required by law or court order

We require our service providers to adhere to strict privacy guidelines and not use your Personal Data for unauthorised purposes.

Should you provide us with another individual's Personal Data, you confirm that:

  • You have obtained consent from that individual prior to disclosing their data to us
  • The Personal Data disclosed is accurate
  • You are validly acting on behalf of that individual and have the authority to share their data

We take all reasonable steps to keep your Personal Data confidential and secure. We will not rent, trade, distribute or sell your Personal Data to third parties without your prior consent, unless permitted by applicable law.

Your Personal Data is stored behind secured networks, with access limited to authorised employees. However, we cannot assume responsibility for unauthorised use wholly attributable to factors beyond our control.

If you choose to remain logged in on the App, anyone with access to your mobile device can access your account — we will not be responsible for any unauthorised transactions arising from this.

We will retain Personal Data as required under PDPA and applicable laws, destroying or anonymising data once it is no longer necessary for its original purpose.

If you have an account, you may access and correct your Personal Data via the Account Settings page. Otherwise, submit a written request to hello@i-ruma.com.

We aim to respond to access requests within 14 days. For correction requests, we aim to correct your data within 14 days of receiving sufficient information. We reserve the right to decline correction requests where exempted under PDPA.

You may withdraw your consent for the collection, use and/or disclosure of your Personal Data by submitting a request to hello@i-ruma.com. Note that withdrawal of consent may result in us being unable to fulfil certain transactions or continue providing certain services to you.

We use a variety of tracking technologies on our Site and App:

  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device to identify you and improve your experience. You may configure your browser to reject cookies, though some features may be affected.
  • Web Beacons: Small graphic images used to track page and advertisement clicks, and email marketing performance.
  • Analytics Tags: Used to analyse usage patterns and customise your browsing experience.
  • Web Server Logs: Records of activity created when webpages are delivered to your browser, including IP address and cookie data.
  • Geo-Location Technologies: Used to send you location-specific offers. You may opt out via device settings.
  • QR Codes: Machine-readable labels used for product tracking, item identification, time tracking, and document management.

During registration, you must provide a password, name, address, and valid email address. It is your responsibility to keep your password strictly confidential.

Our privacy practices will be continuously assessed against new technologies, business practices, and user needs. We reserve the right to change this Privacy Policy at any time and will notify you by posting an updated version on our App and Site.
